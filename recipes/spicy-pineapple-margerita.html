Spicy Pineapple Margarita

Unwind And Spice Up Me Time!

This spicy pineapple margarita brings some jalapeno heat and chili-lime seasoning to your tropical margarita! Use as little or as much as you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime wedge
  • 2 cups ice, or as needed
  • 2 fluid ounces silver tequila
  • 3 jalapeno slices, or more to taste
  • 4 fluid ounces pineapple juice
  • 3/4 fluid ounces triple sec or other orange liqueur
  • 3/4 fluid ounces lime juice

Steps

  1. Sprinkle chili lime seasoning onto a plate. Moisten the rim of a glass with lime wedge. Press the moistened rim into the seasoning. Fill the glass with ice.
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with tequila and 2 jalapeno slices. Use a muddler to crush the peppers. Add pineapple juice, triple sec, and lime juice to the shaker and add 1 cup of ice. Seal and shake vigorously until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds.
  3. Strain margarita into the glass and garnish with remaining jalapeno slice, if desired.
